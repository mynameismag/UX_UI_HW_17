Previous Next
Project Overview

My Role: UX Researcher, UX/UI Designer
Tools: Figma, Miro, and Trello


Five acres website was designed to provide the community family based services (including but not limited foster care, adoption, support groups, etc.) and to generate funding for their cause. However, We have observed that their website’s layout is confusing to users because the organization’s message and goals aren’t clear and is hindering their credibility. Our mission is to redesign their homepage by reconstruct their AI and have created a brand new design system which is more welcoming that help gain traction and build credibility.
