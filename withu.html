WithÜ

Travel Planning Application

Project Overview

My Role: UX Researcher, UX/UI Designer

Travelers are feeling difficult to make plans for group traveling because it is hard to coordinate, collaborate as well as consolidate decisions among groups. WithÜ is a travel mobile app that provide a system that addresses these issues by helping the users coordinate their schedules and consolidate their preferences in order to give user recommendation to make their travel planning easier.
